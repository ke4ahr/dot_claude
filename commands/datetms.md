Run `~/bin/datetms.sh` -- no arguments. Do NOT infer the time.

Outputs: ISO 8601 (e.g. `2026-04-05T19:19:00+00:00`) and filename-safe (e.g. `2026-04-05T19H19M00TZ00H00`). Report both.
